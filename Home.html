Sorting Algorithm Visualizer

Selection Sort

The picky shopkeeper: checks every item and selects the smallest to place in order. Slow but careful.

Insertion Sort

The neat freek: takes one element at a time and places it in the perfect spot. Efficient for small lists.

Bubble Sort

The overly attached friend: swaps neighboring elements repeatedly until the largest floats to the top. Fun but slow!

Merge Sort

The team player: splits the array into halves, sorts them recursively, and merges perfectly. Fast and reliable.

Quick Sort

The ninja slicer: picks a pivot, partitions around it, and sorts recursively. Fast and efficient for large arrays.
